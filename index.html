Welcome to the Los Santos Police Department

The Los Santos Police Department serves and protects the citizens of Los Santos through integrity, professionalism, and dedication to public safety.

Our Mission

The mission of the LSPD is to maintain public order, prevent and investigate crime, and protect life and property while upholding the law with fairness and respect.

Field Training & Professional Standards

  • Structured Field Training Program
  • Professional evaluations and documentation
  • Strict adherence to SOPs and department policy
  • Officer development and accountability

Police Academy

The LSPD Academy prepares recruits with the discipline, tactical knowledge, and decision-making skills required to serve as sworn peace officers in Los Santos.
