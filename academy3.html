LSPD Police Academy

Phase III – Final Academy Review & Setup

Overview

Phase III serves as the final portion of the academy. Cadets will return to the police station to complete setup tasks, receive guidance, and ask any remaining questions. This phase ensures cadets are fully prepared before entering active duty and Field Training.

Training Focus

  • Returning to Station Procedures
  • Keybind and Controls Setup
  • Answering Cadet Questions
  • LSPD SOP Review Expectations
  • Officer Readiness & Professional Conduct

Station Setup & Review

Once back at the station, assist each cadet with setting up their keybinds and controls to ensure smooth and efficient operation while on duty. Take time to answer any questions cadets may have regarding patrol procedures, radio use, or department expectations.

Final Instructions

Cadets are required to study and understand all LSPD Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). Cadets may not leave the station, patrol independently, or clock off duty until they have officially achieved the rank of Officer. Professional behavior and adherence to SOPs are mandatory at all times.

Academy Completion & Cadet Profiles

Once Phase III is complete, select Proceed to Cadet Management to add each graduating cadet to the department roster. Create a cadet profile for every graduate and ensure all required information is accurately entered before assigning them to Field Training.

  • Add new cadet profiles to the system
  • Verify names, callsigns, and academy completion
  • Confirm readiness for Field Training Officer (FTO) assignment
